I build and lead infrastructure platforms at the intersection of telecommunications, enterprise systems, and long‑term resilience.

My work began during the early expansion of the commercial internet, where I founded and operated a regional ISP. That experience shaped how I think about infrastructure today — not as products, but as critical systems built to scale and endure.

In the mid‑2000s, I spent two years working in the music industry, gaining firsthand exposure to content production, media distribution, and the business mechanics behind creative platforms — experience that continues to influence how I think about networks and digital ecosystems.

Today, I lead Varayo, a consolidated telecommunications and infrastructure platform engineered for carrier‑grade performance and durable growth. Pryzm Technologies operates within that ecosystem as an enterprise IT and security arm supporting high‑availability environments.

Beyond infrastructure, I founded Project I For One to advance housing‑first outcomes, and I write at Inkredible Words while pursuing publication.

My career spans the evolution of commercial internet — from early ISP infrastructure to modern carrier‑grade platforms.
